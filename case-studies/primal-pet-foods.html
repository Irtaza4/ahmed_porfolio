01 — The Challenge

A great product with zero search presence

The brand had superior product quality and a loyal customer base — but was effectively invisible online. Organic search delivered almost nothing. Their competitors owned the first page for every high-intent keyword that mattered. The goal wasn't just rankings — it was building a defensible, long-term authority engine.

04
Zero AI Presence
The brand didn't appear in any AI-generated answers or overviews — a growing channel for product discovery that competitors were already leveraging.
02 — How It Was Done

5 phases. 6 disciplines.
18 months of compounding results.

No single tactic wins long-term SEO. This engagement was built as a complete system — each phase unlocking the next, each discipline reinforcing the others. Here's exactly what was executed, when, and what it delivered.

PHASE ONE Technical SEO

Laying the Foundation — Fix What's Broken First

Before any content or link work, the site had to be technically sound. A full audit uncovered 140+ critical issues — crawl budget waste, broken canonical architecture, JavaScript rendering failures, and Core Web Vitals scores in the red across LCP, CLS, and INP. Every issue was triaged, prioritized, and resolved.

Schema markup (Product, Breadcrumb, FAQ, Organization) was rebuilt from scratch. Sitemaps, robots.txt, and internal link architecture were restructured for maximum crawl efficiency. By the end of month 2, the site was clean, fast, and fully indexable — the prerequisite for everything that followed.

140+ Issues Resolved Core Web Vitals — Green Schema Markup Crawl Architecture Canonicalization Log File Analysis Redirect Chain Cleanup Hreflang Implementation
PHASE TWO On-Page SEO

Optimizing Every Page for Searcher Intent

With a clean foundation, the focus shifted to intent-driven on-page optimization. 3,000+ seed keywords were researched, classified by intent type (informational, navigational, commercial, transactional), and mapped to the right pages. Every title tag, meta description, H-tag hierarchy, and internal link across the site was rebuilt — not tweaked, rebuilt.

Product pages went from manufacturer-spec copy to richly optimized, E-E-A-T-compliant content. Image alt text, filenames, and compression were standardized. Featured snippet opportunities were targeted with structured answer content. First organic rankings began appearing by month 4.

3,000+ Keywords Mapped Intent Classification E-E-A-T Signals Title & Meta Rebuild Internal Link Architecture Featured Snippet Targeting Product Page Rewrites Image SEO
PHASE THREE Semantic SEO & Topical Authority

Owning the Entire Topic Universe — Not Just Keywords

This was the highest-leverage phase of the engagement. A full semantic content model was architected — mapping entities, relationships, and subtopics across the entire pet nutrition space. 11 topic clusters were built to saturation: every pillar page, supporting article, FAQ, and semantic variant within each cluster was published and interlinked.

NLP co-occurrence patterns were optimized so content matched how Google's language models understand the topic — not just what keywords appear, but what concepts cluster together. Entity signals, Knowledge Graph reinforcement, and passage indexing were embedded throughout. By month 10, organic traffic was compounding. Google had begun treating the domain as the definitive resource in pet nutrition.

11 Content Clusters Built 80+ Articles Published 400+ FAQ Queries Covered Entity Mapping NLP Optimization Knowledge Graph Signals Content Gap Exploitation Passage Indexing Semantic Silo Architecture
PHASE FOUR Off-Page SEO

Earning Trust — 2,400 Domains, Zero Shortcuts

With topical authority established on-site, the off-page campaign began in earnest. 2,400 referring domains and 15,100 backlinks were earned — not bought — through a multi-channel outreach strategy: digital PR targeting pet industry publications, expert-authored guest content on veterinary and pet care platforms, HARO / media mention campaigns, and broken link building outreach.

Every link was evaluated for domain relevance, editorial context, and traffic quality. A competitor link gap analysis surfaced high-value placements that rivals held but hadn't defended. Toxic link audits ran monthly. By month 15, the Authority Score had climbed to 40 — "Very Good" — and high-competition terms were cracking page one.

2,400 Referring Domains 15,100 Backlinks Built Authority Score: 40 Digital PR Campaigns Guest Posting HARO Outreach Broken Link Building Toxic Link Disavow Competitor Link Gap
PHASE FIVE AI Search Optimization

The Next Frontier — Getting Cited by AI Engines

The final phase tackled what most SEO campaigns completely ignore: AI-generated search answers. Content was restructured for citability — clean answer blocks, entity-rich formatting, and LLM-friendly information architecture that makes it easy for AI systems to surface and attribute the brand.

The results speak for themselves: an AI Visibility Score of 34, achieved from zero — with the brand now surfacing in ChatGPT (255 mentions, 286 cited pages), Google AI Overview (483 mentions, 151 cited pages), Google AI Mode (582 mentions, 178 cited pages), and Gemini (438 mentions, 67 cited pages). As AI search continues to grow, this presence is a compounding moat that competitors can't easily replicate.
